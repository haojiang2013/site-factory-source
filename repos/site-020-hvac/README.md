# ThermoWise — Free HVAC sizing calculator

## What is ThermoWise?

Get a free Manual J load calculation online with no email required. Our tool uses real ACCA standards, not rule-of-thumb. Try it now.

## Why I Built This

Every HVAC sizing calculator tool I tried had the same problems:

- ❌ "Every 'free' Manual J tool I found forces me to enter my email before I can see the load calculation."
- ❌ "Every article just tells me X tons per square foot without asking about my home's insulation or windows."

So I built ThermoWise — a HVAC sizing calculator that actually just works. No email gates, no paywalls, no ads. Just answers.
