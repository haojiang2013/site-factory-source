# MoveWise — Free moving cost calculator

> **Live:** [gomovecalc.xyz](https://gomovecalc.xyz/) · **Free** · **No Signup** · **Open Source**

## What is MoveWise?

Use MoveMath's free moving cost calculator to estimate local & long-distance moves. No email required. Real data from movers, not guesswork.

## Why I Built This

Every moving cost calculator tool I tried had the same problems:

- ❌ "Asks for email and phone before showing any estimate"
- ❌ "Estimates are too low and don’t include packing or stairs"

So I built MoveWise — a moving cost calculator that actually just works. No email gates, no paywalls, no ads. Just answers.

## Features

- ✅ Instant results — no waiting
- ✅ No signup — just use it
- ✅ No email required
- ✅ Real data from industry sources
- ✅ Works on mobile
- ✅ Free forever
