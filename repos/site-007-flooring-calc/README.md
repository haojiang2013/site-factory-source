# FloorWise — Free flooring cost calculator

> **Live:** [floorfound.8zla.com](https://floorfound.8zla.com/) · **Free** · **No Signup** · **Open Source**

## What is FloorWise?

Use our free flooring cost calculator to estimate material, labor, and installation costs for carpet, hardwood, laminate, tile, and more. No email required.

## Why I Built This

Every flooring cost calculator tool I tried had the same problems:

- ❌ "no install cost"
- ❌ "doesnt factor wood species"

So I built FloorWise — a flooring cost calculator that actually just works. No email gates, no paywalls, no ads. Just answers.

## Features

- ✅ Instant results — no waiting
- ✅ No signup — just use it
- ✅ No email required
- ✅ Real data from industry sources
- ✅ Works on mobile
- ✅ Free forever
