# EarthWise — Free garden soil calculator

> **Live:** [soilwise.8zla.com](https://soilwise.8zla.com/) · **Free** · **No Signup** · **Open Source**

## What is EarthWise?

Estimate garden soil volume with our free calculator. Compares bulk vs bagged costs. Works for raised beds, circles, and curved gardens. No email required.

## Why I Built This

Every garden soil calculator tool I tried had the same problems:

- ❌ "estimates are too low because they ignore soil settling and compaction"
- ❌ "inputs reset if I change from feet to inches"

So I built EarthWise — a garden soil calculator that actually just works. No email gates, no paywalls, no ads. Just answers.
