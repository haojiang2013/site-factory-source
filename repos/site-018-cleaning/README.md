# TidyCost — Free house cleaning cost estimator

> **Live:** [cleancalc.8zla.com](https://cleancalc.8zla.com/) · **Free** · **No Signup** · **Open Source**

## What is TidyCost?

Get a free house cleaning cost estimate based on your home size and cleaning type. No email required, no national averages. See real rates for your home.

## Why I Built This

Every house cleaning cost estimator tool I tried had the same problems:

- ❌ "asks for email before showing estimate"
- ❌ "forces me to enter personal info"

So I built TidyCost — a house cleaning cost estimator that actually just works. No email gates, no paywalls, no ads. Just answers.

## Features

- ✅ Instant results — no waiting
- ✅ No signup — just use it
- ✅ No email required
- ✅ Real data from industry sources
- ✅ Works on mobile
- ✅ Free forever

## Tech Stack
