# BuildCost — Free renovation cost estimator

> **Live:** [renowise.8zla.com](https://renowise.8zla.com/) · **Free** · **No Signup** · **Open Source**

## What is BuildCost?

Use our free renovation cost estimator to get localized remodeling prices for kitchens, bathrooms, basements, and whole homes. No email required. Based on real data.

## Why I Built This

Every renovation cost estimator tool I tried had the same problems:

- ❌ "Ask for email and phone before showing results"
- ❌ "Can't select appliance grade"

So I built BuildCost — a renovation cost estimator that actually just works. No email gates, no paywalls, no ads. Just answers.

## Features

- ✅ Instant results — no waiting
