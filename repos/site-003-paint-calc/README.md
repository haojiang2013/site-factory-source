# CoverWise — Free paint coverage calculator

> **Live:** [paintwise.xyz](https://paintwise.xyz/) · **Free** · **No Signup** · **Open Source**

## What is CoverWise?

Use our paint calculator to find exactly how much paint you need. Get gallon estimates for any room. No ads, no location prompts – just fast results.

## Why I Built This

Every paint coverage calculator tool I tried had the same problems:

- ❌ "Forces location/store selection before showing estimate"
- ❌ "Results are vague (e.g., '2-3 gallons') without precision"

So I built CoverWise — a paint coverage calculator that actually just works. No email gates, no paywalls, no ads. Just answers.

## Features

- ✅ Instant results — no waiting
- ✅ No signup — just use it
